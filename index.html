About Us

Light tomorrow with today. 用現在點亮未來。

我們是南臺科技大學五專部，資訊工程科的學生。在這個充滿活力的學術環境中，我們致力於探索資訊科技的無限可能性。 作為資訊工程科的學生，我們得到了專業教師的指導，學習最新的程式設計、網站開發等知識和技能。我們認識到，這些技能不僅僅是課堂上的學習，更是未來實踐的基石。 在校園裡，我們積極參與各種專案和競賽，從團隊合作中學習協調和溝通，從競爭中體驗成長。 在學校，我們不僅僅是學生，更是一個關心彼此、互相支持的社群。我們互相交流學習心得，分享解決問題的方法，一同成長。我們也有機會參與學術活動和社會實習，將所學應用於實際場景，為未來的職業生涯做好準備。 無論是追求創新，還是探索技術的未知，我們在資訊工程科找到了自己的位置。如果你對我們的學習經歷有任何疑問，想知道更多關於我們的故事，歡迎隨時聯繫我們，我們樂意分享我們在學習和成長過程中的經驗。
英文版本
We are students from the Department of Information Engineering at Southern Taiwan University of Science and Technology, College of Technological and Vocational Education. In this vibrant academic environment, we are dedicated to exploring the boundless possibilities of information technology. As students of Information Engineering, we receive guidance from professional instructors and learn the latest knowledge and skills in programming, web development, and more. We recognize that these skills are not only learned in classrooms but also serve as the foundation for future practical applications. On campus, we actively engage in various projects and competitions, learning coordination and communication through teamwork, and experiencing growth through competition. Our learning extends beyond textbooks, seminars, and workshops, constantly expanding our knowledge domains. At school, we are not just students; we are a community that cares for one another and supports each other. We exchange learning experiences, share problem-solving methods, and grow together. We also have opportunities to participate in academic activities and internships, applying what we have learned to real-life situations and preparing for future careers. Whether it's pursuing innovation or exploring the unknowns of technology, we have found our place in the field of Information Engineering. If you have any questions about our learning experiences or want to know more about our stories, please feel free to contact us. We are more than happy to share our experiences in learning and growth.
